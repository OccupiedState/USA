The quietest invasion in history runs on CSV files and federal grants.

Catholic Charities pulled in more taxpayer cash in four years than from collection plates in a century. Endeavors, a company with zero shelter experience, got $600 million in no-bid contracts because its former CEO sat on Biden’s transition team. Average bounty: $6,800 per head delivered alive.

They don’t need tanks. They have Excel.

Every “unaccompanied minor” is a line item. Every got-away is a rounding error. The wall isn’t concrete and steel anymore — it’s a spreadsheet cell that someone quietly changes from red to green.

This is how republics die in the 21st century: not with a bang, but with a pivot table.
